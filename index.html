We Want You!!!

We are offering free listings for all new MINORITY BUSINESSES!

Let us help promote your business. We have created this service to assist minority business owners in the promotion of their business. Our goal is to list every moniority owned business country wide. This service is FREE! We only ask that you tell your friends and customers about our service and give us a like on social media.

All Data Fields Are Required For Each Listing!!!

Once your business listing is submitted you will receive an acknowledgement email. In this email you will see a request for a business logo to be placed with your listing. Your listing will be in the pending status until we receive your business logo image.

You may request via email to deactivate your business listing at anytime. Your listing shall be deactivated within 2 business days upon receipt and acknowledgement of your deactivation request.
